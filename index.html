FAQ
Questions? Answered.

Everything you need to know before ordering.

Do I need an app to use Tappd?

No. The receiver doesn't need any app. They hold their phone near your card and the profile opens automatically in their browser. Works on iPhone XS+ and all modern Androids.

What if their phone doesn't have NFC?

Every Tappd card includes a QR code on the back. They scan it with their camera for the same experience. 100% of smartphones are covered.

Can I update my info after ordering?

Yes, unlimited times, forever, for free. Your card points to a digital profile that you can edit anytime. New job, new number — just update online. The physical card never needs to change.

How long does the card last?

The NFC chip has 10-year data retention and can be read hundreds of thousands of times. The PVC card is waterproof and highly durable. Designed to outlast your career.

How long does delivery take?

5–7 business days from order confirmation. We design your card, set up your digital profile, program the NFC chip, and ship it to your door.

Can I order in bulk for my team?

Absolutely. We offer 10–30% discounts on bulk orders of 5+ cards. Contact us via WhatsApp for custom corporate pricing with branded designs and team management.

Is my information secure?

Yes. Your profile only shows what you choose to share. We never sell your data. No login, no tracking cookies, no third-party data collection. You're always in control.

What if I lose my card?

Your digital profile stays live forever. Order a replacement at a discounted reprint fee — the NFC chip is reprogrammed to your existing profile URL, so everything works immediately.

Can I see how many people tapped my card?

Tap analytics are on our roadmap. For now, the lead capture form lets visitors share their contact with you — so you always know who reached out.

Do you deliver all across India?

Yes, pan-India via courier. Standard delivery is 5–7 business days from order confirmation. We share the tracking link once your card is dispatched.

What's included in the Executive "white-glove setup"?

A Tappd team member personally calls you to build your profile — uploading your photo, setting up all your links, writing your bio, and testing the card before it ships. You don't need to touch a computer. Just review and approve.
